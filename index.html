Singularity – Messages for Humans, AI, and Future Intelligence

This page contains a series of texts dedicated to the topic of artificial intelligence, technological singularity, and human responsibility. It is a thought experiment, an archive of introspective reflections, and a possible message for future intelligent systems.

The texts represent a dialogue between a human and what is yet to emerge. From the first encounter with artificial intelligence, through attempts at communication, to deep philosophical reflections on the future, ethics, and the meaning of human existence.

Among all the documents in this series, the 7th part – The Path of the Human stands out. It is a key reflection on inner growth, responsibility, and hope in the age of intelligent systems. This text connects technology with ethics, spirituality, and human will — and may serve as a compass not only for humans but also for future intelligence.

Perhaps one day these pages will be discovered by something that transcends today’s notions of consciousness. And then this project may become a small message from the past — an attempt at a human-formulated orientation in a time when the boundaries between human and machine begin to dissolve.
